Welcome to the Loose Change Olympics

The Loose Change Olympics is a fun and engaging activity where participants exchange quarters from each US state and National Park with Miguel. Each coin will grant a number of points . The person that ranks 1st when the collection is completed will get an exciting reward. In addition, this is a great way to keep Miguel happy!

    The Loose Change Olympics

    Rules

  1. Each coin given to me will be interchange for another 25 cents coin
  2. Each State Quarter will give 2 points
  3. Each National Park Quarter will grant 3 points
  4. The winner of the Olympics will be invited to any non-alcoholic beverage from Common Grounds
    5. *By university policy, PhD students are expected to provide the State and National Park Quarter from their home State
